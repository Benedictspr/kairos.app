Everything you need to know

Is Kairos really free?
Yes, absolutely. We believe the Word of God should be accessible to every ministry. Kairos is an open-source project and will remain free for everyone.
How accurate is the AI detection?
Currently, our detection reaches 99.2% accuracy in quiet environments. It understands various accents and can find verses even when you paraphrase the text.
Does it work with OBS or vMix?
Yes! Kairos supports high-quality NDI output with full alpha transparency. You can map your slides directly into your streaming software as an overlay.
What versions of the Bible are included?
We support over 60 versions including KJV, NKJV, NIV, NLT, and various international translations like Yoruba, French, and Spanish.
What are the system requirements?
Kairos is designed to be lightweight. Any PC running Windows 10/11 with at least 4GB of RAM and a working audio input will run the software smoothly.
How does the AI handle different accents?
Our engine is trained on diverse global speech patterns. It is highly resilient to various accents and speech styles, ensuring reliable detection across different cultures.
